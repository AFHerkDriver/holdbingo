Fleet Definitions ›
Min Fuel — Fuel to hold at 1,500 ft for 30 minutes plus fly one approach. 757: 4,500 lb · 767: 7,300 lb.

Emergency Fuel — Fuel to initiate a missed approach at 200 ft AFE, climb to 1,500 ft AFE, proceed downwind, and fly another approach from a 10 NM point from the end of the runway; approximately 30 minutes of fuel remaining. 757: 3,500 lb · 767: 5,300 lb.

Final Approach — Fuel to complete a normal approach from the final approach fix. 757: 300 lb · 767: 500 lb.

Go-Around & Return — Fuel including final approach, based on a go-around at 200 ft AFE, climb to 1,500 ft pattern altitude, configuration and speed per go-around procedure, turn to 7 NM final, sea-level standard day, calm wind, max landing weight. 757: 2,500 lb · 767: 3,000 lb.
